What We Treat

Comprehensive Pain Management

We offer a full spectrum of interventional and non-interventional treatments tailored to your condition and lifestyle.

  • Interventional Pain Management

    Minimally invasive procedures targeting the source of pain for lasting relief.

  • Spinal Cord Stimulation

    Advanced neuromodulation therapy that disrupts pain signals before they reach the brain.

  • Joint Injections

    Targeted corticosteroid and hyaluronic acid injections to reduce inflammation and restore mobility.

  • Nerve Blocks

    Precise anesthetic injections that interrupt pain signals along specific nerve pathways.

  • Regenerative Medicine (PRP)

    Platelet-rich plasma therapy harnessing your body's own healing factors to repair damaged tissue.

  • Medication Management

    Carefully monitored, evidence-based pharmacological plans integrated with your overall care.
